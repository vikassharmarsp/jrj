Biography

J Raleigh Jones

Singer, songwriter and TV Show Producer

With singer songwriter J Raleigh Jones, it is all about the music and the song. It is about the awesome true stories he writes into his songs. It is about writing and performing to touch your heart or make you want to dance. It is best said by some of his fans. J.R was born and raised in downstate Illinois. He came from a musical family, and was interested at the age of 8 in writing songs. In high school he played guitar and sang in a 3 piece band at school and church functions as well as a local teen club. As an adult he played in regional bands in central Illinois at clubs fairs festivals and events. But when the country radio station WFMB in Springfield, IL began sponsoring a writers night, he found encouragement from one of the DJ's to pursue his artist career. J.R. obtained a record label deal in Nashville and released an album "Jenny's Eyes" on Castle Records. However he received very limited promotion since the label had a fire and was out of business for over a year. J.R. has had airplay in Europe and Japan, has been on TV in regional country shows in the midwest and deep south. A test was made to determine the reception of J.R.'s music by adding it to a video contest that Music Nation was sponsoring on the internet. It was for videos, but his songs were uploaded to see if they would receive play anyway. During the 30 days in July, he received 19,484 plays with out being in the contest. The 3 songs are on this CD.

Enjoy My Music

J.R. completed his self titled J Raleigh Jones CD in the summer of 2010, and signed with Cashbox Records, a division of the legendary Cashbox Magazine. His first release with the label was "She's a soldier" to the Cashbox reporting stations the first week of October, 2010 and it debuted at number 40 the first week and climbed up the charts to number 10.
